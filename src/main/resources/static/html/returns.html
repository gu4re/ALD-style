Returns


At ALD, we understand that sometimes our customers may need to return or exchange their shoesEntity for various reasons. We want to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases, so we have created the following return policy:

  • Returns and exchanges are accepted within 30 days of purchase.
  • All returned or exchanged items must be in their original condition and unworn, with all original tags and packaging.
  • Customers are responsible for the cost of shipping their returned items to our warehouse.
  • If the return or exchange is due to an error on our part, such as shipping the wrong item, we will cover the cost of return shipping.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned item(s).
  • Exchanges will be processed within 2-3 business days of receiving the returned item(s), and customers will be notified via email with tracking information for the new item(s).

Please note that we do not offer returns or exchanges on items that have been worn or damaged after purchase, except in the case of a manufacturing defect. If you believe your item is defective, please contact our customer service team immediately for assistance.

Thank you for choosing ALD for your footwear needs, and we hope you are completely satisfied with your purchase.
