Order Status


In Zapatillas ALD, we work with Correos to deliver your orders quickly and securely. Once you have made your purchase, we will send you an email with the tracking number of your package so you can stay informed of its location at all times.

Regarding the status of your order, these are the following:

  • Waiting for confirmation: You have just made your purchase and we are reviewing the payment details. We will send you a confirmation email as soon as we have verified everything.
  • In preparation process: We have received your payment and we are preparing your order so that it is ready for shipment. This process can take up to 2 business days.
  • Shipped: Your package has already been delivered to Correos and is on its way to your shipping address. You will receive an email with the tracking number so you can track its location.
  • Delivered: Your order has arrived at its destination! Correos will notify us when they have delivered your package, so don't worry if you don't receive an email from us at this point.


If you have any questions about the status of your order, do not hesitate to contact us through our email or customer service phone number. We are here to help you with anything you need!
